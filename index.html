Frequently Asked Questions

  • Studying abroad is expensive

    Developed countries charge fees as per their living standard. They charge fees propotional to the salary we can get after the course, so we cannot consider it as expensive

  • Its very difficult to get part time jobs abroad

    Unlike India, developed countries encourage students to work part time and earn money They live very independant life, even if they are students. So getting job abroad is easy

  • We cannot earn much doing part time work

    Developed countries ensure a minimum wage varies from 800 INR to 1000 INR per hour for every employees including students. Moreover students can work full time during vacations.

  • Getting scholarship is tough

    UK offers various scholarships varies from 1000 Pounds to 50000 pounds for every students who takes admission in Universities. We ensure students get enough scholarships depending on their academics

  • It is tough to get admission in UK

    Unlike any other country UK does not follow application fee system. So students can apply for more than 5 universities at a time, and we ensure admission for our students.

  • Is it possible to get UK Degree in India?

    Yes. Right. we have tie ups with top-ranked UK universities, so you can study in our Indian campus and get accredited UK MBA with 50% lower cost than studying in UK

  • Can I go to UK for Job after this course?

    Your convocation or Last term will be in UK. So you can visit UK and find a Job there. We provide assistance for placement in India as well as UK. UK marked BREXIT and now follows point based system for migration So it is easier to get a job in UK after your course than ever.
